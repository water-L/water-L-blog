一、Bertrand定理

Among the laws of attraction that assume to be null the action at an infinite distance, that of nature is the only one for which a mobile body arbitrarily launched with a speed less that a certain limit and pulled towards a fixed centre, describes necessarily a closed curve about this centre. All laws of attraction allow closed orbits, but the law of nature is the only one that imposes them.
